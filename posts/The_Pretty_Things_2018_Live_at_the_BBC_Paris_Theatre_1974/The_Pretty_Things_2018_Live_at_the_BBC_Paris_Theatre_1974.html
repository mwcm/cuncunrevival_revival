Remastered rare live show recorded by the BBC at the Paris Theatre, 1974. Available on vinyl for the first time, pressed on limited edition 180g blue vinyl and cut at Abbey Road Studios. Includes a 12-inch insert featuring new liner notes by Mike Stax (editor of Ugly Things magazine).The performance captures the group at the height of their Swan Song era as frontman Phil May leads the band through cuts from 1974's Silk Torpedo album including Dream / Joey and Come Home Momma along with fan favourites such as Old Man Going from the seminal rock concept album S.F.Sorrow (1968).The complete show was cut to vinyl at Abbey Road Studios and is pressed on limited edition 180g blue vinyl. Also included is a 12-inch insert featuring new liner notes by the editor of Ugly Things magazine, Mike Stax.
