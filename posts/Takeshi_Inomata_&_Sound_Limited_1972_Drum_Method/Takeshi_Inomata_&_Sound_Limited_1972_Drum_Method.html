The third album of Takeshi Inomata with his progressive jazz rock band, the Sound Limited featuring pianist Norio Maeda. The line-up consists on the same formation as for the Sound Of Sound L.T.D. record, released two years earlier, except guitarists Ryo Kawasaki & Shinegori Kamiya who left the band. Titles include crossover jazz in the same vein as the Chicago band (Jazz Rock tunes with Funk/Soul inspirations), two amazing Inomata' drum break solos (Drum Concert I & II), Sleeper & Seven Four by Norio Maeda, Pourquoi Pas? by Michel Colombier, and the jazz standards Â Maiden Voyage by Herbie Hancock & Dave Brubeck' Blue Rondo A La Turk. All tracks arranged by Norio Maeda.
