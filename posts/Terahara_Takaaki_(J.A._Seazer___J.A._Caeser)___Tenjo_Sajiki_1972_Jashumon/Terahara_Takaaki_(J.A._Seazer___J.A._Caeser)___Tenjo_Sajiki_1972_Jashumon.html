Recorded live at a theatrical performance by Tenjo Sajiki at Shibuya Kokaido on January 30, 1972. Originally released as part of a 2LP set


This mighty soundtrack for Shuji Terayama's nihilistic movie of the same name contains all the elements necessary to reach J.A. Caesar's intended pleasure centres. Here, turmoil, mind-numbing repetition, abject misery and grisly partriarchs abound, and all orchestrated by Caesar's damaged proto-metal and choral-led psychedelic sound. Mind-infesting in the truest sense, this soundtrack played in the dark is as certified a Gateway to the Underworld as any acknowledged classic by Faust, Magma, the Cosmic Jokers, Ash Ra Tempel or early Amon Düül.

This disc is a spiritual experience, to listen to him you will find yourself upside down in a Japanese theater where the shadows of the musicians will take you on a journey and tormented visionary, ghostly voices and sounds timeless, a solemn ceremony.
