The first title in Yasunori Saito’s exhaustive survey of the music of Masayuki Takayanagi via his jinya disc imprint... the arrival of these discs in the mms catalogue has been a long time in the making; extremely glad to have these seminal documents of free-scrape/noise-guitar crossover available again !!!
