My favourite Caesar album. A mixture of elegance and suspense keeps the listener on her/his toes for most of the album. Like all of Caesar's albums (at least those I've heard thus far) the music is woven out of a soundtrack fabric, and is filled with spoken word parts and extensive façade-like movements which may try a listeners' patience, but all in all the album is very rewarding and warmly recommended.
