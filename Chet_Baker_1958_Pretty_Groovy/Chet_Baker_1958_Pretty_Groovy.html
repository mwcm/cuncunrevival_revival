From the Liner Notes: "Here's Chet again, in pages flashed back from earlier days in the Baker recording history...You'll hear previously unreleased dates..."

Recorded in Los Angeles on July 27, 29 & 30, 1953 (tracks 9-12 & 14), at Radio Recorders in Hollywood on October 3, 1953 (tracks 6 & 8) and October 27, 1953 (track 13), at Capitol Studios in Hollywood on February 15, 1954 (tracks 1, 2, 4, 5 & 7) and in New York City on December 9, 1957 (track 3)
The album compiles tracks previously released on the 1953 10 inch LP Chet Baker Quartet featuring Russ Freeman along with previously unissued recordings.
